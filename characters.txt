Elara is a slender young villager with sun-kissed skin, dark braided hair, and bright green eyes that seem to catch every detail around her. She wears simple linen clothes, often smudged with earth from her explorations. Curious by nature, she possesses a quiet bravery that pushes her to uncover secrets others fear.

Shadow is a sleek, silver-furred wolf with piercing amber eyes that seem to glow in the dark. His movements are silent and precise, blending effortlessly into the forest’s shadows. Fiercely loyal, he possesses a keen instinct for danger, often sensing threats before they appear, making him Elara’s steadfast protector in the Whispering Woods.

At the heart of the Whispering Woods, Elara finally unlocked the secret of the ancient map—it was a guide to a forgotten source of life-giving spring water hidden beneath the forest roots. As she restored its flow to the nearby villages, the land slowly blossomed again, fields turning green and rivers running clear. The once-feared woods became a place of peace and gratitude, and Elara was remembered as the girl who turned mystery into prosperity.

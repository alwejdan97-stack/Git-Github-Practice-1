In the quiet village of Elderglen, where the forest pressed close and the wind seemed to murmur secrets, a young villager named Liora stumbled upon something extraordinary. Hidden beneath loose floorboards in her family’s cottage lay an ancient map, its edges worn and ink faintly glowing as though alive. Strange symbols twisted across its surface, pointing toward the depths of the Whispering Woods—a place no one dared to enter. As Liora traced the markings with trembling fingers, a soft voice seemed to rise from the parchment itself, calling her toward a destiny she could neither understand nor ignore.

Liora sat by the narrow window of her cottage, the fading light of dusk casting long shadows across the map’s surface. The faint glow of its markings pulsed gently, as if responding to her presence. She traced the winding path inked across the parchment, noting how it led deep into the heart of the Whispering Woods—past places she had only ever heard of in hushed stories. The strange symbols seemed almost familiar, tugging at something in her memory she couldn’t quite grasp. When the soft whisper brushed her ears again, she realized it was not coming from the wind outside, but from the map itself.

Her heart pounded, equal parts fear and excitement rising within her. The elders had always warned against entering the woods, speaking of travelers who never returned. Yet, as she looked down at the map, Liora felt a pull stronger than any warning. Carefully, she folded the parchment and tucked it beneath her cloak. With one last glance at her quiet home, she stepped out into the cool evening air, her path already chosen. The Whispering Woods waited—and she would answer its call.
